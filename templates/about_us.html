About Us

At Fumigue Usted, we have revolutionized the way people protect their homes and businesses from pests. Founded in 1991, our company was born with a clear vision: to empower customers to carry out their own fumigations easily, safely, and effectively—without the need to hire a professional exterminator.

Since our inception, we have developed innovative and accessible solutions, providing professional-quality products along with detailed guides so that anyone can apply them with confidence. With over three decades in the market, we have established ourselves as industry leaders, helping thousands of homes and businesses stay pest-free.

Mission

To provide our customers with accessible, safe, and efficient pest control solutions, offering high-quality products and expert guidance so they can confidently fumigate on their own.

Objective

To become the leading company in the DIY (Do It Yourself) fumigation sector by offering innovative products and personalized customer service that ensures satisfaction and safety.

Philosophy

At Fumigue Usted, we believe in autonomy and the power of knowledge. We are committed to educating our customers and democratizing fumigation methods, providing the tools for anyone to protect their space effortlessly.

Our Core Values

  • ✔️ Efficiency – Products with proven results.
  • ✔️ Safety – Solutions designed for both home and professional use.
  • ✔️ Trust – Expert guidance and a commitment to our customers.
